Case Study

Multi-Vendor Marketplace

Project Overview

Built a multi-vendor marketplace enabling independent sellers to list products, manage orders, and receive payouts. Implemented vendor dashboards, commission logic, and dispute handling.

Features

  • Vendor onboarding and KYC flow
  • Commission tiers and payout schedules
  • Vendor-specific shipping and taxes
  • Review and rating system
  • Moderation queue for product approvals

Scalability

Optimized queries, object caching, and CDN-backed media handling to ensure smooth performance with thousands of products and vendors.

Project Details

  • Client: Marketplace Startup
  • Timeline: 16 weeks
